Shades of Pink

All shades of pink are themselves variations on pale red, but they're numerous and distinct all on their own. Pink is an incredibly popular color in both fashion and interior design, and is frequently re-imagined. Some common shades of pink are hot pink, rose pink, coral pink, and salmon pink.
